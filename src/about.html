About this Website

ListHis is a fast, dynamic and private checklist designed to help healthcare professionals efficiently record patients' medical histories.


Based on the chief complaint or associated symptoms entered, ListHis can suggest relevant follow-up questions, ensuring a thorough and targeted history-taking process. All entered information can then be easily exported as a PDF, making documentation and sharing seamless.


ListHis is also static, serverless and runs client-side, ensuring that all entered information is private and secure.


Interested in contributing to ListHis? Check out the GitHub repository!
